CHAT Contacts

What is CHAT?

"CHAT" stands for Crucial Habitat Assessment Tool. CHAT is an online system of maps that displays crucial wildlife habitat based on commonly agreed upon definitions developed by the Western Governor's Wildlife Council. The Western Assocaition of Fish and Wildlife Agencies CHAT provides information across 16 western states, but also links to five state-level CHATs and new state CHATs as they become available.
